Hailing from Johannesburg, Candice Redding has shared the stage with industry sharks such as Martin Garrix, Steve Aoki, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Kygo and many more. Her EDM project as well her underground music alias - Knoxx, seethes with eminence, constitution and experience. In the Indian music scene, DJ Candice has performed at Sunburn, Supersonic, Axe Boat Party, Nat Geo's Wild Wild Fest, Oktoberfest Pune and many more festivals.
