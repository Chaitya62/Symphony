General Rules:

  • Event Category: One team per branch
  • Number of members per team: 3-7. Rotation is allowed.
  • Compulsory participation: A team can comprise of members from any year.
  • Time limit: 20 minutes including sound check, instruments tuning etc.
  • Only basic mic, speakers and a drum kit will be provided to the participants. Amplifiers, instruments, etc, to be brought on your own.
  • Instrumental for 1 min is necessary
