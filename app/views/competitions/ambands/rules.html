General Rules:

  • Team must include a minimum of one vocalist and at least two different instruments ( Different guitars counted separately) and one percussion instrument.
  • Pre recorded music is not allowed.
  • Failure to keep within time limit will result in penalty.
  • Last Date for registration: 29th January ,2017


Elimination Rules:
  • Bands can perform any song of their choice.
  • Time Limit: 15 minutes including setup and clearance and sound check.

Final Rules:
  • Bands can perform any number of songs.
  • Bands have the freedom to perform the songs played in the Elims in the Finals as well.
  • Time Limit: 25 minutes including setup and clearance and sound check.

Judging Criteria:
  • Quality of vocals, quality of instrumental, tightness, sync, choice of song, overall performance, creativity.
