General Rules:

  • Team must consist of a minimum of one vocalist and at least two different instruments (Different guitars counted separately), and one percussion instrument.
  • Pre-recorded music is not allowed.
  • Failure to keep within time limit will result in penalty.
  • Last Date for registration: 1st February, 2017


Elimination Rules:
  • Bands can perform any song of their choice.
  • Time Limit: 15 minutes including setup, clearance and sound check.

Final Rules:
  • Bands can perform any number of songs.
  • Bands have the freedom to perform the songs played during the Elims for the Finals as well.
  • Time Limit: 25 minutes including setup, clearance and sound check.

Judging Criteria:
  • Quality of:
    • Vocals
    • Quality of instrumental
    • Tightness
    • Sync
    • Choice of song
    • Overall performance
    • Creativity
