General Rules:

  • Language of performance should be Hindi/English. However short sub passages in other languages are allowed.
  • Any plagiarized content or any offensive content directed towards any political party, any religion or any community in particular will lead to disqualification.
  • Use of Props is allowed.
  • You are not allowed to play any recorded music using mobile phones or tapes. Only live music. Teams have to bring their own Instruments.
  • Electric instruments are not allowed.
  • Teams are expected to perform in an open air venue, with audience on all sides.
  • Use of any props which affects the stage is not allowed.
  • Teams have to mail the following things at kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu:
    • Synopsis of the play.
    • Production List (List of production/prop material and music instruments brought by the teams).
  • The subject of the mail should be "College Name_Play Name". The deadline for submission is 1st February, 2017 11:59 PM
  • Last Date for Registration: 1st February, 2017.

Elimination Rules:
  • Teams must perform a part of the street play that they will perform in the finals.
  • Time Limit: 10 minutes (including setup and clearance time).

Final Rules:
  • Teams must perform an extended version of the same play which they had performed in the eliminations.
  • Time Limit: 17 minutes (including setup and clearance time).

Judging Criteria:
  • Acting
  • Voice (sync, modulation, diction)
  • Script
  • Crowd Interaction
  • Overall impact
