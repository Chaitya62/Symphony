General Rules:

  • Each team can prepare only one dish which can be an appetizer, a beverage or a dessert.
  • Each team gets to keep all the proceeds from the sales.
  • Each team should expect at least a crowd of 50 people.
  • Teams must carry their own caps, aprons, table cloth and gloves. Caps and aprons are compulsory.
  • Only the team members are allowed to cook. If anybody else is found helping the team in cooking, the team will be disqualified.
  • Incase if team needs any extra ingredients, only one member from your team will be allowed to go out to get it.
  • All the teams must bring their own cooktops, utensils and any other material required. Organisers will not be responsible for any damage to these.
  • Teams have to prepare the same dish during Symphony that they prepared in the elimination round.
  • Last date for registration is 2nd February,2017.

Elimination Rules:
  • The teams have to prepare the dish earlier and bring it to the venue. However there will be an oven provided if the dish needs to be served hot.
  • There will also be a question-answer round based on the dish prepared.

    • Final Rules.
  • Time: 1 hour will be allotted for preparation and 4 hours for serving the food.
  • Each team will be provided 2 Plug Points and a Work Table.
  • Ingredients to be brought by the participants.

Judging Criteria:
  • 50% on Sales, 50% on judges' Scores.
  • Judges' scores will be based on:
    • Taste of dish
    • Marketing
    • Cleanliness
    • Presentation of dish
    • Sales of dish
