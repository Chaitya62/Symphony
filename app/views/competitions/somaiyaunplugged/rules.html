Elimination Rules:
  • The maximum duration of the rendition should not exceed 6 minutes.
  • File size should not exceed 15MB

Final Rules:
  • Time Limit: 8 minutes including sound setup and clearing.
  • Multiple songs can be sung.
  • If any instrumental audio is required then the participant should get it in a pendrive in .mp3 format only.
  • No instruments will be provided by us.
  • Acoustic instruments are allowed.
  • No electric instruments are allowed.
  • Five mics in all will be provided for the vocalist and instruments.

Judging Criteria:
