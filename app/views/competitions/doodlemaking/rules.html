General Rules:

  • The doodle can be a cohesive design, or doodling can be used to create portraits, animals, objects,landscapes or text, the patterning conveying texture and dimension.
  • Theme: An Odyssey of Dreams.
  • All the work must be original.
  • All entries must be submitted as digital files. There is no limit to the number of entries and it must be mailed to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu under the subject "Doodle Making" along with:
    • Name
    • Phone Number
    • College Name
  • Entries should be last submitted by 5th February, 2017.

Final Rules:
  • Artist are required to use only a black pen, permanent marker, or a sharpie and a paper(minimum A4).
  • Theme is open to interpretation.
  • Image requirements: JPG/JPEG file.

Judging Criteria:
  • Creativity
  • Theme incorporation
  • Doodle relevance
  • Complexity
  • Neatness
  • Overall Impact.
