General Rules:

  • Crews are allowed to participate, provided all participating members are college students, albeit not necessarily from same college.
  • Participants may use more than one song in their performance.
  • Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object is not allowed on stage.
  • Pre-recorded music should be brought in a pen drive in .mp3 format only.
  • Last date for registration: 29th January, 2017
  • Genre: Open

Eliminations Rules:
  • Teams are supposed to send a video of their complete performance in .mp4 format or a youtube link on kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu
  • The video should not necessarily be the one the team is going to perform.
  • Time Limit: 4-12 minutes.

Finals Rules:
  • Censor round will be held on the day of the event prior to the event.
  • Usage of props is allowed. These have to be brought by the teams themselves but the organizers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
  • Time Limit: 12mins
  • Teams must carry at least one backup of their pre recorded music.
  • Time limit must strictly be observed. Failure to keep within the time limit will result in a penalty.
Judging Criteria:
  • Choreography, Energy, Synchronization, Stage utilization, Costumes, Innovation, Overall impact.
