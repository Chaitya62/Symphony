General Rules:

  • Each Participant is allowed to send in more than one entry. Participants are not allowed to collaborate and send in a common entry.
  • The story must be written in English.
  • No form of plagiarism will be tolerated. The entry must be an unpublished work.
  • An Alternate ending is necessary.Participant may select any movie/sitcom/book/anime from the given list. Please refer to the website www.kjsce-symphony.org for the list.
  • Illustrations are not allowed.
  • Participants are allowed to create upto 2 new characters provided the characters are given a proper background.
  • For Sitcoms the alternate ending can be done to any season/episode.

Final Rules:
  • Entries must be emailed as PDF file to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu under the subject “Alternate Ending”. Along with the name ,part/episode/season of the movie/ sitcom/ book/ anime.
  • Last date for submission of entries: 5th February, 2017.
  • Word Limit: Minimum: 300 ; Maximum : 2000
  • Email must contain name of participant, email address, college name, phone number and title of the entry.

Judging Criteria:
  • Originality of Idea, Execution, Form (Language, Narrative Structure)
