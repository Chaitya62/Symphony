General Rules:

  • Participants can send word document containing only text, describing your photo or thematic interpretation.
  • Participants are allowed to adjust the hue/saturation, brightness/contrast of a picture. Photo-morphing, manipulations or local editing is not allowed.
  • Photos containing the credentials of a participant in the picture or the frame will not be considered.
  • Photographs without EXIF data will not be accepted.

Final Rules:
  • Theme: Forced Perspective.
  • Theme is open to interpretation.
  • The edited photograph(if any of the above mentioned are done) should be uploaded on facebook tagging “KJSCE Symphony” and “KJSCE Shutterbugs” as well as mailed to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu under the subject”Photography” along with the original photograph.
  • Last date to submit the photograph is 5th February,2017.
  • There will be 2 prizes based on judges choice and people’s choice.
  • People’s Choice will be awarded on basis of maximum Likes.

Judging Criteria:
  • Relevance to Theme, Creativity, Visual Impact.
