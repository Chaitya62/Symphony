General Rules:

  • Participants can perform anything ,on exceeding the time limit there will be a penalty.
  • Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object(which may be dangerous) is not allowed on stage.
  • Participants have to mail the following things at kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu
  • The type of talent and Prod List (List of production/prop material and music anything else brought by the participants ). The subject of the mail should be “Somaiya’s Got Talent”. The deadline for submission is 1st Feb, 2017 11:59 PM

Elimination Rules:
  • Time Limit: 3mins
  • Participants can perform a part of their final sequence for elims.

Final Rules:
  • Time Limit:7mins including stage setup and mic check if required.
  • No instruments will be provided by us.
  • A maximum of 5 mics will be provided.

Judging Criteria:
  • Stage Presence, Innovation, Energy,Overall Impact
